About Me

Student. Teacher. Musician.

I'm currently studying Pre-Business at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah. My academic journey has taken me from Timpview High School to BYU–Hawaii and now to BYU Provo, shaped along the way by meaningful experiences in service, hospitality, and music education.

From 2024–2025 I served as a missionary for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, an experience that deepened my German language skills and instilled in me a greater capacity for empathy and leadership. Today I continue that spirit of service as a piano instructor at Slope School.

Outside the classroom I am a cellist, pianist, and singer — music is both my creative outlet and one of my greatest joys.
